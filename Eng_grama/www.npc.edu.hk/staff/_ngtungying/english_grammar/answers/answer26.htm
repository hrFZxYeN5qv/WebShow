Lesson 26

【供參考】 
1. This child will be twelve years old.
2. Shall I send this letter for you?
3. My uncle will come from America next week.
4. Shall he do this thing?
5. Will he so so?
6. I will visit him tomorrow.
7. Shall I play ball with your son?
8. You shall not stay here.
9. Will you be at leisure tonight?
10. I will be in America in a few days.
