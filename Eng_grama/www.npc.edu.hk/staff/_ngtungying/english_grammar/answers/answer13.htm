Lesson 13

【供參考】
1. Can your bird sing?
2. Does his sister go to school?
3. This novel of yours is very interesting.
4. These white rabbits are not ours.
5. I like my dog but he does not like his.
6. Is that dog in the kennel yours?
7. I have not hers but I have mine.
8. Your lilies smell very sweet but mine do not smell sweet.
9. Do you have their books?
10. These are theirs.
