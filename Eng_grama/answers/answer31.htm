Lesson 31

【供參考】
1. The old man who sits under the tree is a scientist.
2. He has lost the money which I gave him.
3. These are the books which I read last year.
4. That man whose hair is golden is an Englishman.
5. The horse which I ride on is a good one.
6. The cup which my servant broke belonged to Miss. Lee.
7. The girl whom I like best is your sister.
8. I will buy you all the things which you need.
9. The reason why he did that is very obvious.
10. This is the place where I lived last year.
