Lesson 14

【供參考】
1. I see an English teacher in the school.
2. Is this young Japanese a student?
3. Is that old man at the door a German?
4. Those are Italian merchants.
5. Can those three French women write Chinese?
6. Your horse is an English one and mine, a French one.
7. Are you and he Italians?
8. His grandfather lives in Switzerland.
9. Do you read Chinese classics?
10. Is that red-dressed girl on the grass a teacher of French?
